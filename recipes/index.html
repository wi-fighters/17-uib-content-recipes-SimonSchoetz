Pumpkin Soup Receipe

Ingredients

  • 1 Hokkaido pumpkin
  • 100 ml cream
  • 1 tablespoon Sherry
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 Onion
  • 500ml vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • A little bit of sugar
  • Peper
  • Salt
  • Grated nutmeg

Preparation

  1. Get rid of the pumpkin seeds and make dices
  2. Make onion into dices
  3. Put it into a pan with some butter for a couple minutes
  4. Put some sherry into it
  5. Put the vegetable broth into it
  6. Cook it for 20-30 minutes
  7. Add cream and curry powder
  8. Add salt, sugar peper and nutmeg as you like it
  9. Enjoy you motherfucker!
